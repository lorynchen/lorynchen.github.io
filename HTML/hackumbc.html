Key Takeaways

Organizing a hackathon was an amazing and fun exerpience! I had the wonderful opportunity of leading the design efforts to rebrand our hackathon each season and helping promote our brand to the hacker community.

I learned how important communication is when working within a team. Our team used Slack on the daily to keep everyone on the same page. We also had weekly meetings where everyone gave updates on what they were working on for the event.
