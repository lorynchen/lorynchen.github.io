Our Brand

Versatile logo

Our logo


Our logo is a full representation of our quirky selves. The rectangular shape represents our organization as we are a house of designers. The slant in the "house" is to draw more attention to us and to resemble how unique we are. The color variations is a metaphor for how different our designers are from each other.

We are made up of photographers, artists, animators, and creators of all levels.
